GAMEPLAY
RULES

Each contestant will be given a bank of 250 dollar credits.

All contests will be 10 credits per player for a total of 20 credits in the pot (minimum wager).

Every contest will be subject to a 7% rake deducted at the close of the contest. If a player loses their credits and are unable to make the minimum wager of 10 credits, they may request a new deposit of 250 credits to their account. There are no limits to the number of times a player may replenish their account.

Each game will be a 5 minute Blitz format. Each player will have a total of 5 minutes to move their pieces.

Each game will require a 10 credit wager from each player.

If a player’s time expires, they lose the match. 

Each contest must have a winner. There are no draws.

If a player cancels the game or exits the game before completion, they lose.

If a game expires in a perceived stalemate, the game engine determines which player holds the advantage, (able to mate) and is declared the winner. 

In the unlikely event of a draw, each player will receive a credit to their account of the amount wagered, minus the rake of 7%.

If the ChessKings anti-cheating system detects and raises suspicion of cheating or artificial assistance, the player is subject to being disqualified at the sole discretion of ChessKings.
